My Belief

A Fierce Competition: 'Neijuan'

'Neijuan' is a Mandarin word. If translated directly, it is 'inward rolling'. To understand this, imagine the front row audience in a theater stand up for a better vision (but not really better since they are already at the front). Then the back row audience are blocked and have to stand up as well. In the end, everyone is worse off.
This can be a projection to current society, for example, unnecessary education barriers to get employed. As a social planner, how can we make our lives easier?
